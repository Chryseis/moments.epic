Moments of Happiness is a side project made by EPIC Agency.
A series of WebGL experiments developed to make you smile (and spin your fans) using Three.js and GSAP Library.
Source codes are available on codepen for educational purposes only.
These experiments are not fully optimized and might not work on some browsers or devices.
